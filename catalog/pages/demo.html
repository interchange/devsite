Interchange Live Demo

Our demonstration store gives you an idea of what __IC__ can do for you. This store template is what most people base their sites on, but it is by no means all that Interchange is capable of!

Public storefront
Back-office administration

To access the administration section of the demo store, enter username demo and password demo when prompted.

The demo is completely modifiable. You can access the administration section and change any aspect of the store.

The store may be broken in various ways if users try changing different settings. Store data, including pages, items, pricing, and orders, will automatically reset to the defaults periodically.

The demo runs the latest nightly build of Interchange.
