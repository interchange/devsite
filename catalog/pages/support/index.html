Interchange Support

__IC__ has a wide variety of community and commercial support options available for users. The [page users/community]Community page details the support options available to users from the Interchange community, including mailing lists and IRC.

Commercial support is available from many companies providing professional services. A listing of companies that provide hosting with Interchange access is also available.

  • See [page support/commercial]Commercial for a list of __IC__ support companies
  • See [page support/hosting]Hosting for Interchange-enabled hosting arrangements

    • You could also post commercial __IC__ support, hosting and quote requests on our business mailing list ([filter mailto]interchange-biz+icdevgroup.org[/filter]).
